AeroGuard Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-10-12

This Privacy Policy explains how AeroGuard ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use the AeroGuard mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described here.

Contact

If you have questions or requests about this Policy, contact us at: support@yourcompany.com

Information We Collect

  • Information You Provide: Account info (email/password, Google, or anonymous), optional profile preferences and health-related sensitivities you choose to store.
  • Location Information: Precise location (GPS, Wi‑Fi, cellular) to center the map, show your position, and provide AQI-aware routing. Foreground location permission is requested; background location is not requested unless explicitly enabled later.
  • Device and App Information: Device model, OS, app version, language, region; diagnostic logs and crash reports.
  • Usage Information: In‑app interactions (map opens, taps, route selections).
  • Notification Identifiers: Push token to deliver optional notifications (if enabled).
  • Third‑Party Services: Firebase (Authentication/Database), Google Maps Platform/react‑native‑maps, Expo services. See their policies: Firebase, Google, Expo.

How We Use Information

  • Provide and improve App functionality (maps, AQI-aware routing, location features)
  • Authenticate users and personalize content
  • Send optional notifications (if enabled)
  • Monitor performance, troubleshoot, detect/prevent abuse, and ensure security
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce terms

Legal Bases (where applicable)

We process data based on consent (e.g., location, notifications), contract (to provide the App), legitimate interests (security, improvement), and legal obligations.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share with service providers (Firebase, Google, Expo), for legal reasons, or as part of a business transfer.

Data Retention

We retain information as needed to provide the App, comply with laws, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You can request deletion (see Contact).

Your Choices and Rights

  • Location: Manage in device settings. App may default to a fallback location if disabled.
  • Notifications: Enable/disable in the App or device settings.
  • Access/Correction/Deletion: Contact us to request.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information. If your audience includes children, update this policy and ensure compliance (e.g., COPPA).

Changes
